The South African 4×4 & Outdoor Show is a jam packed event of high quality and will be setting the benchmark in the 4×4 & Outdoor industry.

This is a very unique show that offers live entertainment on stage, stunts, demonstrations like bike, quad and vehicle demonstrations at various points of the venue.

This highlight of the 2014 show will be the live monster truck show. The public will also have the once in a lifetime opportunity to take a ride inside a monster truck.
